Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how HomeAxis collects, uses, shares, and protects personal information when you use our website, submit property enquiries, schedule visits, and make booking token payments.

1. Information We Collect

  • Identity and contact data: name, phone number, email address.
  • Property preference data: location, budget, unit type, and related enquiry details.
  • Transaction metadata: property selected, booking amount, timestamp, status.
  • Technical data: device/browser details, IP, usage logs, and cookies.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Respond to enquiries and coordinate site visits.
  • Process booking token workflows and customer support.
  • Send service updates and requested property information.
  • Improve platform security, fraud prevention, and analytics.
  • Meet legal, tax, accounting, and compliance obligations.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers (for example, Stripe). Card and UPI credentials are handled by the payment provider on their secure systems. HomeAxis does not intentionally store complete card numbers, CVV, or full payment credentials on this website.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data only with service providers, legal authorities (when required), and property stakeholders involved in your requested transaction flow, subject to applicable law.

5. Consent and Legal Basis

By using the website and submitting forms/payment consent, you authorize HomeAxis to process your personal data for enquiry handling, booking operations, and related communications.

6. Retention

Data is retained for business and legal purposes only as long as necessary, then deleted or anonymized where feasible.

7. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, update, or deletion of personal information by contacting us.
